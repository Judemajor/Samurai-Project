Books

  1. Musui's Story: The Autobiography of a Tokugawa Samura (Covers the not so noble parts of being a lower class samurai)
  2. Lust, Commerce, and Corruption (A book giving a very detailed account of the changes taking place in the samurai era)
  3. Tour of Duty by Constantine Vaporis (Very detailed look at samurai during 17th through 19th centuries. Follows the lives of the daimyo)
  4. Performing the great peace by Luke Roberts (Takes a philosophical view of the samurai class and how it affected society)
  5. The Taming of The samurai by Eiki Ikegami (Takes place during the Tokugawa period and views how the warrior regime functions)
